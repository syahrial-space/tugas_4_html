Dovizioso: the attendance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years - the third highest on the grid behind Valantino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP). and Dani Pedrosa(Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-year-old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - Marc Marquez'(Repsol HOnda Team) most consistent challenger.
